Every physical component attached to a vehicle during assembly is logged here — one row per part per vehicle. This is effectively the Bill of Materials (BOM) in transactional form. It records which supplier provided the part, how long installation took, its cost, and whether a defect was flagged during fitment.

Each vehicle passes through multiple inspection stations before it can be marked complete. A quality check row records one test at one station — paint finish, structural integrity, emissions, electrical systems, or safety systems. If a test fails, the defect code, rework time, and inspector are logged for accountability and warranty traceability.
